## üîç Problem Statement
In today's competitive job market, HR departments face significant challenges:

- **Volume Overload:** HR professionals spend countless hours screening hundreds of applications for each position
- **Consistency Issues:** Manual screening can lead to inconsistent evaluation criteria across candidates
- **Time Constraints:** The pressure to fill positions quickly can result in overlooking qualified candidates
- **Unconscious Bias:** Human reviewers may inadvertently bring personal biases into the screening process
- **Resource Intensive:** Traditional interview processes require significant time and human resources
- **Candidate Experience:** Delayed responses and lack of feedback negatively impact candidate experience

Our AI-powered solution addresses these challenges by automating the initial screening process, ensuring fair evaluation, and significantly reducing the time-to-hire while maintaining high-quality candidate selection.

## üéØ Our Solution
We're developing an intelligent HR filtering system that:
- Automates initial candidate screening using advanced LLM technology
- Provides consistent evaluation criteria across all applications
- Reduces time-to-hire by up to 75%
- Minimizes unconscious bias in the selection process
- Improves candidate experience through quick feedback
- Allows HR professionals to focus on high-value interactions

## ü§ñ Key Features
- **Intelligent Resume Parsing:** Extract and analyze key information from resumes
- **Automated Initial Screening:** Use LLMs to evaluate candidate responses
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Understand candidate communication style and soft skills
- **Custom Interview Generation:** Create personalized interview questions based on job requirements
- **Bias Detection:** Implement fairness checks in the filtering process
- **Performance Analytics:** Track and analyze recruitment metrics
